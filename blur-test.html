Troubleshooting:

  • If you see blur effects: Your browser supports backdrop-filter! The WheelGen overlay should work perfectly.
  • If you don't see blur effects: Your browser doesn't support backdrop-filter. The overlay will use fallback solid backgrounds.
  • Common issues: Some browsers require hardware acceleration or specific settings to enable backdrop-filter.
